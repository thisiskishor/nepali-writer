# Example 6: Register Variations

Same message in three registers: formal, semi-formal, and informal.

---

## Context

The message: "I want to ask you to help me with a project."

---

## 1. Formal / Official (औपचारिक)

```
आदरणीय महोदय/महोदया,

उपर्युक्त विषयमा निवेदन गर्न इच्छा गर्दैछु कि मेरो एक प्रकल्पमा 
तपाईंको सहयोग गरिदिनु हुने छ कि छैन भनी अनुरोध गर्दछु।

यदि सम्भव भए कृपया सहयोग गर्नुहोस्।

सादर,
[Name]
```

### Characteristics

- **Pronoun:** तपाईं (formal, respectful)
- **Verb forms:** गर्नुहोस्, हुनु (formal imperatives)
- **Tone:** Respectful, formal, written
- **Vocabulary:** विषय (topic), सहयोग (cooperation), अनुरोध (request)
- **Structure:** Complete sentences, formal transitional phrases
- **Greeting:** आदरणीय (honorable), महोदय/महोदया (sir/madam)
- **Closing:** सादर (respectfully)

### When to Use

- Government correspondence
- Official letters
- Business proposals
- Academic submissions
- Legal documents
- Professional requests requiring high formality

---

## 2. Semi-Formal (अर्ध-औपचारिक)

```
नमस्कार,

मलाई एक प्रकल्पमा काम गर्न खोज्दैछु। यदि समय छ भने, तपाईंले 
मलाई सहयोग गर्न सक्नुहुन्छ कि? तपाईंको सहायता भएमा राम्रो हुन्थ्यो।

धन्यवाद र शीघ्र जवाफको प्रतीक्षा गर्दैछु।

शुभकामना सहित,
[Name]
```

### Characteristics

- **Pronoun:** तपाईं (respectful but less formal than formal register)
- **Verb forms:** Mix of formal imperatives and conversational forms
- **Tone:** Polite, direct, balanced formality
- **Vocabulary:** Neutral Nepali (not too formal, not too casual)
- **Structure:** Clear sentences but more natural flow
- **Greeting:** नमस्कार (hello/namaste)
- **Closing:** शुभकामना सहित (with best wishes)

### When to Use

- Business emails between colleagues
- Professional requests to acquaintances
- Formal but friendly communication
- Emails to professors/mentors
- Professional networking

---

## 3. Informal / Conversational (अनौपचारिक)

```
नमस्कार साथी,

हे, मेरो एक प्रकल्पमा काम गर्दैछु। तिमी सहयोग गर्न सक्छौ कि? 
तिमीलाई समय छ भने एकपल मेरो साथ काम गर।

धन्यवाद!

[Name]
```

### Characteristics

- **Pronoun:** तिमी (informal, familiar)
- **Verb forms:** Conversational imperatives (गर, सक्छौ)
- **Tone:** Casual, friendly, personal
- **Vocabulary:** Simple, everyday Nepali
- **Structure:** Short sentences, informal sentence connectors
- **Greeting:** नमस्कार साथी (hello friend)
- **Closing:** Just name or informal thanks

### When to Use

- Conversations with friends and peers
- Informal emails among close colleagues
- Personal requests
- Social communication
- Casual writing

## Honorific System Comparison

### The Three Levels

| Level | Pronoun | Verb Form | Use Case | Formality |
|-------|---------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| **Formal** | तपाईं | गर्नुहोस्, हुनुहुन्छ | Government, officials, strangers | ⭐⭐⭐ High |
| **Semi-formal** | तपाईं/तिमी | गर्नुहुन्छ, सक्नुहुन्छ | Colleagues, acquaintances | ⭐⭐ Medium |
| **Informal** | तिमी/तँ | गर्, सक्छौ | Friends, close relationships | ⭐ Low |

### Key Differences

| Aspect | Formal | Semi-formal | Informal |
|--------|--------|-------------|----------|
| **Pronoun** | तपाईं | तपाईं or तिमी | तिमी or तँ |
| **Verb suffix** | -नुहोस्, -हुन्छ | -हुन्छ, -गर्न सक्नु | -ो, -छौ |
| **Nouns** | Formal equivalents | Mixed | Casual |
| **Sentence structure** | Complex, formal | Balanced | Simple, direct |
| **Transitions** | Formal phrases | Natural connectors | Casual connections |

## Verb Conjugation Examples

### "To do" (गर्नु) in Three Registers

| Register | Form | Meaning | Use |
|----------|------|---------|-----|
| **Formal** | गर्नुहोस् | please do (formal imperative) | Direct requests to officials |
| | गर्नुहुन्छ | does (formal present) | Formal statements |
| **Semi-formal** | गर्नुहुन्छ | do (polite present) | Regular communication |
| | गर्न सक्नु | can do (polite) | Polite requests |
| **Informal** | गर् | do (direct imperative) | Friends, close people |
| | गर्छौ | are doing (casual) | Conversational |

## Complete Message Variations

### The Setup
Person A wants to ask Person B for help with a project.

### Version 1: To Government Official
```
आदरणीय श्रीमान्/श्रीमती,

मेरो एक जरुरी प्रकल्पमा तपाईंको सहयोग आवश्यक छ। यदि अनुकूल 
हुन सके, कृपया सहयोग गर्नुहोस्।

आभारी हुँ,
[Name]
```

### Version 2: To Manager/Mentor
```
नमस्कार,

मैले एक प्रकल्पमा काम शुरु गरेको छु। यदि समय हुन सके, तपाईं मेरो 
साथ सहयोग गर्न सक्नुहुन्छ कि?

धन्यवाद,
[Name]
```

### Version 3: To Close Friend
```
हे साथी,

मेरो एक प्रकल्पमा तिमी मेरो साथ काम गर न। सजिलो र मजेदार हुन्छ।

धन्यवाद!

[Name]
```

## Register Selection Guide

**Ask yourself:**

1. **How well do I know this person?**
   - Strangers/officials → तपाईं (formal)
   - Colleagues/acquaintances → तपाईं/तिमी (semi-formal)
   - Friends/close people → तिमी (informal)

2. **What is the context?**
   - Official/government → Formal register
   - Professional but friendly → Semi-formal
   - Personal → Informal

3. **What is the topic?**
   - Serious/important → Tend toward formal
   - Routine/casual → Can be more informal
   - Mixed situation → Semi-formal is safest

4. **What is the medium?**
   - Written letter → More formal than emails
   - Email → Somewhat formal
   - Text/chat → More informal

---

## Common Mistakes

❌ **Mixing registers inconsistently:**
- "तपाईं यहाँ बस ।" (formal pronoun + informal verb)
- Fix: Either "तपाईं बस्नुहोस्" or "तिमी बस"

❌ **Using formal register when not needed:**
- Writing to a friend with government-official language
- Fix: Match register to relationship

❌ **Too casual in professional settings:**
- Using informal pronouns with business contacts
- Fix: Use semi-formal or formal register

✓ **Consistency:** Maintain chosen register throughout the message

---

## Key Takeaway

**Register = Relationship + Context**

The register you choose tells the listener/reader how much respect/formality the situation deserves. Match your register to the person and context, and maintain consistency throughout.
