# Example 1: Formal Business Letter

English → Nepali translation of a professional deadline extension request.

---

## English (Input)

```
Dear Mr. Sharma,

I am writing to request an extension on the deadline for the project submission. 
Due to unforeseen circumstances, our team needs an additional two weeks to 
complete the work to your satisfaction.

We assure you of our continued commitment to quality and timely delivery. 
Please advise if this arrangement is acceptable.

Sincerely,
Rajesh Thapa
Project Manager
```

---

## Nepali (Output)

```
आदरणीय श्रीमान् शर्माजी,

उपर्युक्त विषयमा यस कार्यालयमा निवेदन दिनु परेकाले सादर निवेदन गर्दछु कि प्रकल्प
प्रस्तुत गर्ने समयसीमा अलिकति बढाइपाऊँ भनी विनती गर्दैछु। अप्रत्याशित परिस्थितिले गर्दा
हाम्रो टोलीलाई काम सन्तोषजनक रूपमा सम्पन्न गर्न थप दुई हप्ता आवश्यक भएको छ।

हामी गुणस्तर र समयमै डेलिभरीको प्रति आफ्नो प्रतिबद्धता निरन्तर रहेको कुरामा 
आश्वस्त गर्दछौं। यस व्यवस्था स्वीकार्य छ वा छैन भनी कृपया सूचित गर्नुहोस्।

भवदीय,
राजेश थापा
परियोजना प्रबन्धक
```

---

## Translation Notes

### Register & Tone
- **Register:** Formal (तपाईं + गर्नुहुन्छ forms)
- **Tone:** Respectful, professional, requesting
- **Structure:** SOV maintained throughout

### Key Translation Decisions

| English | Nepali | Why |
|---------|--------|-----|
| "Dear Mr. Sharma" | आदरणीय श्रीमान् शर्माजी | Formal honorable address (आदरणीय) + respectful title (श्रीमान्) |
| "I am writing to request" | सादर निवेदन गर्दछु कि | Direct formal request form (more natural in Nepali) |
| "Due to unforeseen circumstances" | अप्रत्याशित परिस्थितिले गर्दा | Causal construction (cause + verb suffix -ले गर्दा) |
| "our team" | हाम्रो टोलीलाई | CORRECTION: Originally "आमाद टोलीलाई" — fixed to "हाम्रो" (our) |
| "Two weeks" | दुई हप्ता | Modern Nepali for week is हप्ता (not सप्ताह) |
| "Sincerely" / "Yours faithfully" | भवदीय | Standard formal letter closing |

### Grammar Points Demonstrated

1. **Honorific Matching:** तपाईं (formal pronoun) + गर्नुहोस्/गर्दछौं (formal verbs)
2. **SOV Structure:** Sentence elements follow subject-object-verb order
3. **Formal Vocabulary:** कार्यालय (office) not अफिस, प्रकल्प (project) not प्रोजेक्ट
4. **Postposition Attachment:** परिस्थितिले (postposition -ले attached), समयसीमा (postposition -मा attached)
5. **Danda Punctuation:** Each sentence ends with दण्ड (।) not Latin period

### Vocabulary Choices

- **गुणस्तर** (quality) — formal; alternatives: गुण, गुणवत्ता
- **डेलिभरी** (delivery) — transliterated; formal context accepts loanword
- **प्रतिबद्धता** (commitment) — formal Nepali; alternatives: वचनबद्धता

### Register Markers

- आदरणीय (honorable) — opens formal letters
- सादर निवेदन (respectful request) — formal politeness
- भवदीय (yours faithfully) — formal closing
- निरन्तर (continuously) — formal/literary

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid (in this context)

❌ **Wrong:** तिमी ... गर्नुहोस् (informal pronoun + formal verb = mismatch)
✓ **Correct:** तपाईं ... गर्नुहोस् (formal pronoun + formal verb = consistent)

❌ **Wrong:** हाम्रो टीम (mixing transliterated English)
✓ **Correct:** हाम्रो टोली or हाम्रो दल (Nepali terms for team)

❌ **Wrong:** It is a project. (SVO word order)
✓ **Correct:** यो एक प्रकल्प हो। (SOV word order, "this one project is")

❌ **Wrong:** आदरणीय श्रीमान शर्मा (missing जी for respectful address)
✓ **Correct:** आदरणीय श्रीमान् शर्माजी (जी adds respect)

---

## When to Use This Translation Style

- Government and official correspondence
- Formal business proposals
- Legal documents
- Academic submissions
- Professional requests requiring high formality
